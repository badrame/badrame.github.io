PROJECTS

Oona the Druids Path

Steam Page

Oona The Druids Path is a 2.5D Platformer videogame developed in Unreal Engine 4.
Developed with a studio inside the university composed by programmers, artist and musicians.
Work as programmer, programmer team coordinator and game designer.

Currently released on Steam for free and its receiving good reviews.
It has been nominated for the GDWC in the fan category.


Some of my contributions are:

Player Movement Mechanics

WallJump in some surfaces.
Second jump on Jump Balls.
Slide and dash under some objects.
Glide before picking up an object.

Camera System

Dynamic changes on distance and velocity.
Able to follow player, lock on a position,
follow with differents weights between different objects
or follow a spline.

Game Interaction System

Player can interact with buttons and preassure plates with the environment.
Work for doors, moving platforms, rotation actors and more.
Event Synergy with the camera system.
Easy scalable to the other systems of the game.

Game Feel Work

Player movement hacks like Ghost Jump and Input Execution delay.
Sound effects implementation and camera shakes.
Particles design and implementation.
Level Scripting for light and post process changes and adjustments.

Game and Level Design

Enemy behaviour and player mechanics design contributions.
Level Boss and Final Boss design and implementation.
Last level zone design.
Contributions on the rest of the level designing and scripting.

Optimization and other stuff

Profiling with the GPU visualizer and the different UE4 visualation options.
System of activation and deactivation animations and ticks.
Building and packaging debugging.
Game dead loop.
